WAX STAMP - LIKE A ROYAL

Seal it with more than just a kiss!

This is beginner’s class. A bit artsy and crafty, approx 2 hours.

I will teach you several techniques and tricks. I provide many wax colours, and many stamp motives. Also some specials like petals, golden leaf, ribbons,…

You may stamp a letter, invitations, a box, a bag, a bottle, a gift, a painting, your journal,… That is up to you to bring (up to 5). Best holds on paper, but also can be nice on textile and glass.

Some stamps and waxes can be bought after class.
