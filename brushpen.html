MODERN BRUSH PEN LETTERING

Brush lettering is playful, popular, and can bounce around.

This is beginner's group class, approx. 3 hours.

I will teach you how to use and care for the tools, brush drills that build the letters, the alphabet, and some curls.

We will use special brush pens, appropriate papers, and my alphabet workbook.

You get to take the workbook and basic art supplies home, as well as your artwork.
