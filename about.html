COPENHAGEN CALLIGRAPHY

The visual art of writing is a synonym for calligraphy. That perfectly describes the focus of our company called Copenhagen Calligraphy.

Thanks to our employees' skills and passion for this type of art, we grew the number of clients, as boutique shops, banks, luxury hotels etc. Now we are selling our talent in different countries. Thank you notes, invitations and many more are decorated by handwritten words that give the feeling of elegance. People, interested in calligraphy can also take part in workshops, and learn the beauty of the hand lettering.

CURLY LETTERS, DIP PENS, GOLDEN INKS ARE OUR PASSION

I am Anita, a Czech - Dutch based in Copenhagen.

I have previously exhibited in galleries throughout Europe and I am the main artist for Copenhagen calligraphy.

With a degree in Diplomacy and Business Law and passion for art, I now give you elegant ways for calligraphy to connect customers with your brand.
